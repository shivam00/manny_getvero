All your data and insights in one place

Stitch delivers your data to your data warehouse. With all your data in one place, you can perform any analysis and ask any question of your data using your preferred analytics tool.

What is Stitch?

Stitch is a modern extract, transform and load (ETL) platform for rapidly moving data from it's origin source to data warehouses such as Amazon Redshift, Google BigQuery, Snowflake and others.

Connecting Axtrion and Stitch helps your team make better decisions

  • Stitch data together

    Axtrion's email campaign reporting is powerful, but there are certain reports that can only be generated when you bring your data together. Combine data to get better insights.

  • Custom reporting

    Send data via Stitch to your data warehouse and create custom internal and external reports with popular charting tools like Mode Analytics, Looker and Periscope Data.

  • A historical record

    By warehousing the email interactions that originate in Axtrion you can backup and store a gospel record of important customer interactions.

  • Don’t write your own ETL scripts.

    Writing middleware to capture webhooks and load data into your warehouse is complex and time-consuming. Stitch handles the hard work for you, enabling you to get up and warehousing faster.

Meaningful and actionable insights

Axtrion’s integration with Stitch allows you to extract, transform and load (ETL) your email campaign data into your data warehouses, such as Redshift and Bigquery.

Joining up your email and push interaction data with the rest of your customer data will give you access to meaningful and actionable insights. For example, by combining your data from payment apps, like Stripe, with your data from Axtrion, you can analyze conversions and revenue from email campaigns.
