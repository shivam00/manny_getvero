Get your data into Axtrion

      
Connect to your real time event stream in Segment, push real time events via Axtrion's own libraries, upload ad hoc CSVs and pull in data from your own APIs. Data is critical for segmentation and email personalization, so when it comes to data we've got your back.

      
    
        
  • 
          
          
    Works with Segment
    If you’re already using segment, sending event data to Axtrion is as simple as flipping a switch.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
          
    Understand users and events
    Axtrion understands data about users and their actions ("events"). We cache this data to enable powerful segmentation and automation.

Modelling

    

      
View and model your customer data in one place

      
We provide a scalable system to turn your customer data into meaningful segments. Forget "lists", forget manual maintenance of user states and remove scattered segmentation from your application and backend architecture. Use Axtrion to design segments via our UI and store them centrally in one place.

      
    
        
  • 
          
          
    Powerful segmentation
    Create, save and deploy completely dynamic groups of your customers based on their attributes and actions.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
          
    Customer profiles
    Troubleshoot, investigate and deep dive into your customer lifecycle. View all of the data Axtrion knows about each of your customers.

Craft any email automation you can dream up using Axtrion's powerful tools: schedule one-off campaigns, recurring campaigns (hourly, daily or weekly), design drip and automated campaign workflows and trigger transactional emails, all in one place.

Deployment

    

      
Scalability and centralised reporting

      
Deploy your email automation and content reliabily whether it's to one or 100+ million customers. Let us handle deliverability or BYO email infrastructure platform – we'll track and centralise your reporting.

      
    
        
  • 
          
          
    BYO email infrastructure
    
          
    Maintain your email reputation and control your deliverability by leveraging one or more of the most popular email infrastructure platforms.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
          
    Data warehouse every interaction
    
          
    Output data about every email interaction to your own API for data warehousing using webhooks. We also support Segment's Sources to export data with a single click.

Here's a complete list of our most popular features

    
    
      
  • Scheduled Newsletters
    Send one-off campaigns to individual segments – instantly, or at a scheduled time in the future.
    • 
      
  • Detailed Reporting
    Get aggregate and day-by-day breakdowns of every email campaign sent through Axtrion. Plays nice with data warehouses too.
    • 
      
  • Liquid Content
    Personalise every email using the Liquid templating language and utilise your customer data.
    • 
      
  • Manage HTML templates
    Upload, organise and edit your fully responsive email designs and reuse them in all of your campaigns.
    • 
      
  • Visual Editor
    Define areas of your email content that the whole team can edit in Axtrion's point-and-click content editor.
    • 
      
  • Send per Timezone
    Schedule your emails at the right time for every customer, no matter where in the world they are.
    • 
      
  • Track Conversions
    Go beyond just clicks and track the true success of each campaign using user actions as converion goals.
    • 
      
  • A/B Testing
    Multi-variate test the subject, from address, content and template for any email campaign, including triggered workflows.
    • 
      
  • CSV Imports
    Hit the ground running and import your customer attributes and actions using Axtrion's CSV import tools.
